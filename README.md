# Welcome to "NetCrawl"

"NetCrawl" is an exciting web-based roguelike game with procedurally generated levels and a variety of characters. Embark on a unique adventure!

## About the Project

In "NetCrawl," you forge your own path through a world of procedurally generated levels, where each level offers a unique adventure. One of the game's key features is the ability to switch between different characters, each with their own skills and unique items, adding depth and diversity to your gaming experience.

## Features

- Procedurally generated dungeon levels: No two levels are the same, ensuring endless variety during gameplay.
- Multiple base characters with different gameplay and unique items: You can choose which character to play and switch between them as you progress.
- **Combination of Two Game Styles**: "NetCrawl" seamlessly combines both Top-Down View and Side-Scrolling View, providing a diverse and engaging gaming experience.
- Web-based roguelike: "NetCrawl" delivers a unique roguelike experience right in your web browser.
